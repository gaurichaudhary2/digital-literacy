Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Safety Tips for College Students

* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails/messages
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking
* Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
* Do not share personal information (OTP, passwords, ID details) with anyone
* Verify websites before entering login or payment details

UPI / Financial Safety

* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
* Always verify payment requests before approving transactions
* Avoid scanning unknown QR codes
* Use only trusted and official payment apps

Report Cybercrime

* Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline Number: **1930**

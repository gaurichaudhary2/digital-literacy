Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

Do's

* Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts
* Verify information before sharing it online
* Use strong privacy settings to protect your data
* Share positive and meaningful content
* Think before posting anything publicly

Don'ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
* Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
* Do not spread misinformation or fake news
